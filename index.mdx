## Get Started in 3 Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download or Open AfuChat">
    Install AfuChat from the Google Play Store or open [afuchat.com](https://afuchat.com) in your browser — no installation required on the web.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up with your email address, set a unique @handle, and personalize your profile with a photo and bio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Connecting">
    Search for friends, join communities, send your first message, or ask AfuAI anything — you're ready to go.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  AfuChat is free to download and use. Premium features and ACoins can be purchased to unlock additional perks.
</Note>
