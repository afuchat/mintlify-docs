The Discover tab is where AfuChat helps you grow your world beyond the people you already follow. It combines a personalized content feed, short-form video, a people search, and smart user recommendations — all in one place. Whether you're looking for a specific person or simply want to see what's popular right now, Discover is your starting point.

## The Discover Feed

The feed inside Discover is split into two tabs:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="For You" icon="sparkles">
    A personalized stream of posts from across the AfuChat community, ranked by AfuChat's feed algorithm. The algorithm factors in your past likes, follows, and interactions to surface content that matches your interests. The more you engage, the more tuned it becomes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Following" icon="user-group">
    Shows posts only from the accounts you follow, in reverse-chronological order. This tab is available when you are signed in. If you haven't followed anyone yet, AfuChat will prompt you to explore suggested users.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

When new posts arrive while you're reading the feed, a floating pill appears at the top of the screen. Tap it to jump to the newest content without losing your scroll position.

## Search

Tap the **search** icon (magnifying glass) in the Discover header to open the Search screen. You can look up any AfuChat user by:

- **@handle** — type the exact handle (e.g. `@alex`) to find an account directly.
- **Display name** — search by first name, last name, or any part of a person's display name.

Tapping a result opens that user's public profile, where you can follow them, send a message, or view their posts.

## Shorts

Shorts is AfuChat's short-form video experience. Tap the **Shorts** pill in the Discover header to enter the full-screen vertical video player. Videos play automatically as you scroll up through the feed.

Inside the Shorts player you can:

- **Like** a video by tapping the heart, or double-tap the screen for a quick like.
- **Comment** on a video using the speech-bubble button on the right rail.
- **Share** a video to other apps with the share button.
- **Follow** the creator directly from the video without leaving the player.

Tap the back arrow or swipe down to return to the Discover tab.

## User Suggestions

AfuChat surfaces people you may want to follow in two ways:

1. **Suggested Users strip** — a horizontal carousel of profile cards shown at the top of the For You feed. Each card shows the person's avatar, display name, and a Follow button. Suggestions are based on mutual connections, shared interests inferred from your activity, and trending accounts.
2. **User Recs cards** — recommendation cards that appear inline in the feed at regular intervals, showing a small cluster of suggested accounts you can follow without leaving the feed.

Dismiss any suggestion by tapping **×** on the card if it isn't relevant to you.

<Note>
  If an account is set to **private**, you will see a lock icon on their profile and your follow request will be held for their approval. Their posts won't appear in your feed until they accept. Private accounts still appear in search results and suggestions, but their content is hidden until you're approved as a follower.
</Note>
