Your AfuChat profile is the public face of your account — it's what other users see when they tap your name in a chat, discover you in search, or scan your digital ID card. Taking a few minutes to complete your profile also improves how you appear in recommendations and the "People You May Know" feature.

## What's on your profile

| Field | Details |
|---|---|
| **Display name** | The name shown next to your avatar. Can be changed once every 7 days. |
| **@handle** | Your unique username (e.g. `@alex_afu`). Used for mentions, search, and your QR card. Can be changed once every 30 days. |
| **Bio** | A short description of up to 150 characters. Supports plain text; an AI-generate button is available to draft one for you. |
| **Avatar** | A square profile photo cropped to 1:1 aspect ratio. |
| **Cover photo** | A wide banner image cropped to 3:1 ratio, shown at the top of your profile page. |
| **Website** | An optional link displayed on your profile. |
| **Country, gender & birthday** | Optional personal details that help tailor your experience. |
| **Interests** | Up to 18 topic tags (pick at least 3) that shape your Discover feed and help others find you. |

## How to edit your profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Edit Profile">
    Go to your profile tab (the **Me** tab), then tap the **Edit Profile** button. You can also reach it from **Settings → Edit Profile**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your avatar or cover photo">
    Tap the camera icon on your avatar or the banner area at the top of the screen. Choose a photo from your library. Avatars are cropped to a 1:1 square; banners are cropped to a 3:1 landscape ratio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your name and handle">
    Tap the **Name** or **Handle** field under the Identity section. AfuChat checks handle availability in real time as you type — a green checkmark confirms the handle is free.

    If you changed either field recently, a lock icon will appear and show how many days remain before you can change it again (7 days for display names, 30 days for handles).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write or generate your bio">
    Tap the **Bio** field and type up to 150 characters. Tap the **✦ AI Generate** button to have AfuChat's AI draft a bio based on your display name, interests, and country — you can edit the result before saving.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add optional details">
    Fill in your **website URL**, select your **country** from the searchable list, choose your **gender**, and set your **birthday** using the day/month/year pickers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick your interests">
    Scroll to the Interests section and tap the topics that apply to you. Select at least 3 to unlock the full profile-completeness score and improve your feed recommendations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Tap **Save** in the top-right corner. Any updated avatar or banner is uploaded first, then all changes are saved together.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile completeness

AfuChat shows a **Profile Completeness** card at the top of the Edit Profile screen with a percentage bar and a checklist of five items: profile photo, display name, bio, interests (at least 3), and country. Completing all five makes your profile eligible to appear in the "People You May Know" discovery section.

## Digital ID card

Every AfuChat account comes with a **Digital ID card** — a personal QR code that encodes your profile link. Others can scan it to open your profile instantly without searching.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find your QR card">
    Tap your avatar or the share icon on your profile page to open the Digital ID card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share or scan">
    Show your QR on screen for someone to scan, or tap **Share** to send it as an image. To scan someone else's card, tap the scan icon in the top bar of the Chats or Discover tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Prestige level and verified badge

AfuChat awards prestige levels based on your activity, engagement, and time on the platform. Higher prestige unlocks cosmetic perks and may display a special badge on your profile. A blue **verified badge** is available to notable public figures and creators through a separate verification process.

<Tip>
  Choose a memorable @handle early — something short, easy to spell, and closely related to your name or brand. Because handles can only be changed once every 30 days, it's worth settling on one you'll want to keep long-term. Other users will @mention you by your handle in posts and comments, so clarity matters.
</Tip>
