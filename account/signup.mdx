AfuChat is free to join and takes under a minute to set up. Whether you prefer signing in with a social account or a private email address, you have two options to get started — and your account works across iOS, Android, and the web from day one.

## Sign-up methods

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Email & password" icon="envelope">
    Register with any email address. AfuChat sends a 6-digit OTP to confirm your address before your account is activated.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Continue with Google" icon="google">
    Use your Google account for one-tap sign-up and sign-in. No separate password needed.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Create an account with email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the sign-up screen">
    Launch AfuChat and tap **Create account** on the welcome screen, or tap **Continue with email** if you prefer not to use Google.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email and password">
    Type your email address and choose a password of at least 8 characters. Tap the eye icon to reveal your password as you type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept the terms">
    Check both boxes to confirm you are **13 years of age or older** and that you agree to AfuChat's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Both are required to proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Create account">
    AfuChat creates your account and immediately sends a **6-digit verification code** to the email address you provided.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Enter the 6-digit code in the verification dialog. If the code doesn't arrive within a minute, tap **Resend code**. The code expires after a short window, so verify promptly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    Once your email is confirmed, AfuChat walks you through the onboarding flow where you'll pick your @handle, add a photo, and choose your interests.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sign in to an existing account

On the login screen you can identify yourself using any of the following:

- **Email address** — the address you registered with
- **@username** — your unique handle (without the `@`)
- **Phone number** — your registered mobile number in international format (e.g. `+1 555 123 4567`)

AfuChat detects the format you type and routes your login correctly — you don't need to select an account type.

On supported devices, AfuChat also offers **Face ID / Touch ID** sign-in after your first password login. The biometric option appears automatically on the sign-in screen whenever a stored session is available.

## Reset a forgotten password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap Forgot password?">
    On the email sign-in screen, tap the **Forgot password?** link below the password field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email">
    Provide the email address associated with your account and tap **Send reset code**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the reset code">
    Check your inbox for a 6-digit code. Enter it in the next step along with your new password (minimum 6 characters). Confirm the new password and tap **Update Password**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with your new password">
    After a successful reset you are signed out automatically. Return to the sign-in screen and use your new credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your **@handle** must be unique across all AfuChat accounts. Handles can contain letters, numbers, and underscores, with a minimum of 3 characters. You choose your handle during onboarding after registration, and you can change it once every 30 days from your profile settings.
</Note>
