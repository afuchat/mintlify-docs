AfuChat's messaging experience puts every conversation tool in one place — from quick text replies to voice messages, file attachments, and red-envelope ACoin gifts. Every message delivers in real time, and a smart offline queue means your words are never lost, even when your connection drops.

## Starting a New Chat

To open a fresh 1-on-1 conversation:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Chats tab">
    Tap the **Chats** icon in the bottom navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the compose icon">
    Tap the **New Message** button at the top right of the Chats screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the person you want to message">
    Type a name or **@handle** in the **To:** field. AfuChat searches your followed contacts first, then the full user directory. You can also scan a contact's **QR code** using the QR icon in the top-right corner of the New Message screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap their name to open the conversation">
    Your conversation opens immediately. If no conversation exists yet, AfuChat creates one automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can also start a chat directly from any user's profile page — tap **Message** to jump straight to your conversation.
</Tip>

## Message Types

AfuChat supports a rich set of content types in every conversation:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Text" icon="message">
    Type and send plain text. AfuChat supports basic markdown-style formatting — wrap text in `**asterisks**` for bold or `_underscores_` for italics.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Voice Messages" icon="microphone">
    Hold the microphone button in the chat bar to record a voice message. Release to send instantly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Images & Video" icon="image">
    Tap the attachment icon to pick a photo or video from your gallery. Videos can be trimmed in-app before sending.
  </Card>
  <Card title="File Attachments" icon="paperclip">
    Share documents, PDFs, and other files from your device using the document picker in the attachment menu.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Voice messages are a fast way to express nuance or tone when typing feels too slow. The recipient can listen at any time, even offline, if the message has already downloaded to their device.
</Tip>

## Reactions and Replies

**Reactions** — Long-press any message to open the reaction picker. Tap an emoji to react; tap it again to remove your reaction. Reaction counts are visible beneath each message.

**Replies** — Swipe a message to the right (or long-press and choose **Reply**) to quote it. Your new message threads visually below the original, making it easy to follow multi-topic conversations.

## Read Receipts and Typing Indicators

AfuChat keeps you in the loop about the conversation in real time:

- **Delivered** — A single checkmark appears once your message reaches AfuChat's servers.
- **Read** — A double checkmark appears when the recipient opens the conversation and views your message.
- **Typing indicator** — When the other person is composing a reply, their name appears with a live typing animation at the top of the conversation.

## Red Envelopes

Send ACoin gifts wrapped in a digital red envelope right inside any chat:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the attachment menu">
    Tap the **+** or attachment icon in the message bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Red Envelope">
    Select **Red Envelope** from the menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the amount and a note">
    Set how many ACoins you want to send and add an optional message. In a group chat, you can also set how many members are allowed to claim the envelope.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    Tap **Send Red Envelope**. The envelope appears in the chat — the recipient taps it to claim their ACoins directly to their wallet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Message Search

To find a specific message in any conversation:

1. Open the chat.
2. Tap the **search** icon at the top of the screen.
3. Type a keyword — AfuChat highlights matching messages and lets you jump to each one.

## Offline Mode

AfuChat is built to work even when your connection is unreliable. When you are offline:

- An **Offline** banner appears at the top of the screen.
- Any message you send is added to a **pending queue** stored on your device.
- The moment connectivity is restored, AfuChat automatically syncs and delivers all queued messages in order.

Previously loaded conversations and messages remain readable from your local cache while you are offline.
