Channels are AfuChat's broadcast feature. Unlike group chats, where every member can send messages, a channel has a single owner who publishes posts to any number of subscribers. Channels are ideal for creators, businesses, and communities that want to push updates, announcements, or content to a large audience without the back-and-forth of a group conversation.

## Creating a Channel

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to New Message">
    Tap the **Chats** tab, then tap the compose icon at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap New Channel">
    Tap **New Channel** in the quick-actions section at the top of the New Message screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the channel details">
    Enter a **channel name** (required), an optional **description**, and tap the avatar circle to upload a channel photo from your gallery.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set visibility">
    Toggle between **Public** (anyone can find and subscribe) or **Private** (only people with the link can subscribe).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the checkmark">
    Tap the **checkmark** button at the top right to create your channel. You are automatically subscribed as the owner.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Subscribing to a Channel

To follow a channel and receive its broadcasts:

1. Find a channel via **Search** or through a shared link.
2. Open the channel page.
3. Tap **Subscribe**. The channel is added to your chat list and new broadcasts appear there in real time.

To unsubscribe, open the channel and tap **Unsubscribe**.

## Posting a Broadcast

Only the channel owner can publish posts. To send a broadcast:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your channel">
    Tap the channel from your chat list or from your profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compose your message">
    Type your broadcast text in the composer at the bottom of the channel screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach an image (optional)">
    Tap the image icon to attach a photo from your gallery.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Send">
    Tap the send button. Your broadcast is delivered instantly to all subscribers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  For longer or more carefully crafted broadcasts, use the dedicated **Broadcast** screen. Tap the compose icon at the top of your channel page to access a full-screen editor where you can write text and attach an image before publishing.
</Tip>

## Verified Channels

Channels can be verified by AfuChat, which displays a **verified badge** next to the channel name. Verification signals to subscribers that a channel represents an authentic creator, business, or organisation.

Verified status is awarded through AfuChat's premium subscription and verification review process. When a channel is verified:

- A blue verified badge appears next to the channel name in search results and on the channel page.
- Subscribers see the badge in their chat list alongside the channel name.

## Channels vs. Groups

| | Channels | Groups |
|---|---|---|
| **Who can post** | Owner only | All members |
| **Typical size** | Unlimited subscribers | Focused membership |
| **Best for** | Announcements, content, updates | Team chat, coordination |
| **Subscriber can reply** | Can like and comment on posts | Yes, everyone messages freely |

<Note>
  Only the channel owner can send broadcasts. Subscribers can like and comment on posts, but cannot send their own broadcasts into the channel. If you want full two-way conversation, use a [group chat](/messaging/groups) instead.
</Note>

Like groups, the number of channels you can *create* is tied to your subscription tier. Free accounts can create one channel, Silver up to three, Gold up to ten, and Platinum has no limit. You can subscribe to as many channels as you like on any plan.
