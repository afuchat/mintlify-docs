Stories are short-lived posts — photos or videos — that you share with your followers and that disappear automatically after 24 hours. They appear as circular avatars at the top of the Discover and social feed screens, giving your audience a quick, immersive way to see what you are up to right now. Stories are a great way to share moments, behind-the-scenes content, or quick updates without adding permanent posts to your profile.

## Creating a Story

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap your avatar or the Story button">
    On the **Discover** screen or social feed, tap your own avatar (with the **+** indicator) or tap **Add Story** to open the story creator.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your media">
    You have two options:
    - **Camera** — Tap the camera icon to shoot a new photo or video on the spot.
    - **Gallery** — Tap the gallery icon to pick an existing photo or video from your device library.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a caption (optional)">
    Type an optional caption of up to 200 characters beneath the media preview. You can also tap the emoji icon to insert an emoji into your caption.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set privacy">
    Tap the **privacy** selector and choose who can see this story (see [Privacy Settings](#privacy-settings) below).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Share">
    Tap the **Share** button to publish. AfuChat uploads your story in the background, so you can keep using the app while it processes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Story Duration

Every story expires automatically **24 hours** after you publish it. Once a story expires, it is removed from your profile and is no longer visible to anyone. You cannot extend a story's lifetime, but you can re-share the same content as a new story at any time.

## Privacy Settings

You control who sees each story individually when you post it:

| Privacy Option | Who can see the story |
|---|---|
| **Everyone** | All of your followers |
| **Close Friends** | Only people on your close friends list |
| **Only Me** | Only you — useful for drafts or private memories |

<Tip>
  Your privacy setting is chosen per story, not globally. If you want most stories to be public but occasionally share something more private, simply switch the setting before you tap Share. You do not need to change any account-wide preference.
</Tip>

## Viewing Others' Stories

Stories from people you follow appear as circles at the top of the **Discover** screen and social feed. Circles with a coloured ring have new, unviewed stories.

When you open a story:

- **Auto-advance** — AfuChat automatically moves to the next story in the sequence once the current one finishes.
- **Tap to skip** — Tap the right side of the screen to jump to the next story, or tap the left side to go back to the previous one.
- **Hold to pause** — Press and hold anywhere on the story to pause the timer and read at your own pace.
- **Swipe down to close** — Swipe down to exit and return to your feed.

A progress bar at the top of the screen shows how far through the current story you are and how many stories remain in the sequence.

## Story View Counts

After you publish a story, you can see exactly how many people have watched it:

1. Open your own story by tapping your avatar.
2. Swipe up on the story, or tap the **eye** icon at the bottom of the screen.
3. A panel slides up showing the **total view count** and, when available, a list of individual viewers with their names and the time they viewed.

View counts update in real time as more people watch your story.
