Group chats on AfuChat let you bring several people together in a single conversation thread. Whether you are coordinating with a team, planning an event, or keeping a friend circle connected, groups give everyone a shared space to message, react, share files, and more — with the same real-time features you get in 1-on-1 chats.

## Creating a Group

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the New Message screen">
    Tap the **Chats** tab, then tap **New Message** at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap New Group">
    On the New Message screen, tap **New Group** in the quick-actions section at the top.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a group name">
    Type a name for your group. A clear, descriptive name helps members identify the chat at a glance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select members">
    Scroll through your followed contacts and tap each person you want to add. You need to select at least one contact to create the group.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Create">
    Tap **Create** in the top-right corner. AfuChat creates the group and opens the conversation immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can also pre-select contacts from the contact list by long-pressing a name to enter multi-select mode, then tap **New Group** in the action bar that appears at the bottom.
</Tip>

## Adding and Removing Members

Group admins can manage membership at any time from the group settings screen:

**To add members:**

1. Open the group conversation.
2. Tap the group name or photo at the top to open **Group Info**.
3. Tap **Add Members** and select from your followed contacts.
4. Tap **Add** to confirm.

**To remove a member:**

1. Open **Group Info**.
2. Find the member you want to remove.
3. Long-press their name (or tap the options icon next to them) and choose **Remove from group**.

<Note>
  Only admins can add or remove members. The person who created the group is automatically an admin.
</Note>

## Admin Roles and Permissions

Every group has at least one admin. Admins can:

- Add new members to the group.
- Remove existing members.
- Promote other members to admin.
- Demote admins back to regular members.
- Edit the group name, photo, and description.

**To toggle admin status for a member:**

1. Open **Group Info**.
2. Tap the member whose role you want to change.
3. Toggle the **Admin** option on or off.

## Group Info and Settings

Tap the group name or avatar at the top of the conversation to open the **Group Info** screen, where you can:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Edit Group Name" icon="pencil">
    Rename the group at any time (admins only).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Change Group Photo" icon="image">
    Upload a photo from your gallery to use as the group's avatar (admins only).
  </Card>
  <Card title="View Members" icon="users">
    See a full list of everyone in the group, their handles, and their admin status.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Leave Group" icon="arrow-right-from-bracket">
    Leave the conversation at any time from the Group Info screen.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  The number of groups you can create depends on your subscription plan. Free accounts can create one group. Silver members can create up to three, Gold members up to ten, and Platinum members have no limit. There is no cap on the number of groups you can *join*.
</Note>
