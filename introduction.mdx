AfuChat is a mobile-first super app built for people who want everything in one place. Whether you're catching up with friends over chat, exploring a social feed tailored to your interests, asking an AI assistant for help, or sending a virtual gift to someone special — AfuChat brings it all together in a single, fast, and easy-to-use app. It's available free on Android and on the web at [afuchat.com](https://afuchat.com).

## What Makes AfuChat Different

AfuChat is not a single-purpose tool — it is a super app designed around how you actually live and communicate.

- **Everything in one session.** You don't need to switch between a chat app, a social app, a payments app, and an AI tool. AfuChat keeps all of these connected under one account and one interface.
- **AI built into your conversations.** AfuAI is available the moment you sign up. It lives inside your chat list just like any contact, so help is always one tap away.
- **A social economy you earn from.** Nexa XP is earned naturally as you use the app — post, chat, complete achievements — and can be converted or spent on things you actually want.
- **Real-world services from your phone.** Mini Programs connect you to airtime top-ups, hotels, event tickets, and bill payments, reducing the number of apps you need installed.
- **Personalised from day one.** During sign-up you choose your interests, and AfuChat uses them immediately to shape your Discover feed, game recommendations, and more.

## Platforms

AfuChat is available on **Android** (via the Google Play Store) and on the **web** at [afuchat.com](https://afuchat.com). Your account, messages, wallet, and profile stay in sync across both platforms automatically.

<Note>
  AfuChat is free to download and free to use. Premium subscriptions and ACoin top-ups are optional and can be purchased inside the app.
</Note>
