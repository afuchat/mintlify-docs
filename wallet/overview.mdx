AfuPay is your in-app financial hub inside AfuChat. From a single screen you can check your balances, top up with a card or mobile money, send and receive currency between friends, and track every transaction — no third-party app required.

## Your two currencies

AfuChat uses two distinct currencies. They serve different purposes and are not interchangeable.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="ACoin (AC)" icon="diamond">
    ACoin is AfuChat's purchasable virtual currency. **1 ACoin = \$0.01 USD.** Use ACoins to send money to friends, buy virtual gifts, subscribe to Premium, unlock prestige upgrades, play games, buy airtime and data through mini programs, and send Red Envelopes in chat.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Nexa (XP)" icon="bolt">
    Nexa — displayed as XP — is earned by staying active on AfuChat: posting stories, chatting, referring friends, and more. Nexa reflects your status and level on the platform and can also be transferred to other users.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## The Wallet home screen

When you open AfuPay you'll see:

- **ACoin balance** — your current spendable balance in AC and its USD equivalent
- **Nexa (XP) balance** — your earned activity points
- **Transaction history** — a running log of every top-up, send, receive, subscription payment, gift, and Red Envelope

The wallet is available on the **Apps** tab in the bottom navigation. Tap the wallet icon to open AfuPay.

<Note>
  AfuPay is a **virtual currency wallet**, not a bank account or regulated financial product. ACoins hold no monetary value outside of AfuChat and cannot be withdrawn as cash. Keep your account secure — AfuChat support will never ask for your password or payment credentials.
</Note>
