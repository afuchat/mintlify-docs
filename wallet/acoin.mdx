ACoins (AC) are the spendable virtual currency that powers transactions inside AfuChat. You purchase them through AfuPay, receive them from other users, and spend them across everything from Premium subscriptions to virtual gifts. Think of ACoins as a universal token for the AfuChat ecosystem.

## Exchange rate

**1 ACoin = \$0.01 USD.** The rate is fixed, so the math is always simple:

| ACoins | USD equivalent |
| ------ | -------------- |
| 100 AC | \$1.00 |
| 500 AC | \$5.00 |
| 1,200 AC | \$12.00 |
| 2,500 AC | \$25.00 |

When you top up in a local currency (KES, UGX, NGN, and more), AfuPay converts the amount to USD at the current exchange rate, then credits the corresponding number of ACoins.

## How to earn and receive ACoins

- **Top up** — purchase ACoins directly through AfuPay using MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, or a debit/credit card
- **Receive from others** — when a friend sends you ACoins, they appear in your balance instantly
- **Accept a money request** — fulfilling a payment request from another user credits their account and debits yours
- **Receive a Red Envelope** — claim your share from a Red Envelope in a chat

## Nexa (XP) vs ACoins

It helps to understand how these two currencies differ:

| | ACoins (AC) | Nexa (XP) |
|-|-------------|-----------|
| **How you get it** | Purchase or receive from others | Earn through app activity |
| **Exchange rate** | 1 AC = \$0.01 USD | No cash equivalent |
| **Primary use** | Spending — gifts, premium, transfers | Prestige — levels, status |
| **Can you send it?** | Yes | Yes |
| **Can you cash out?** | No | No |

<Warning>
  ACoins are **non-refundable virtual currency**. Once purchased, ACoins cannot be converted back to cash or reversed. Transactions such as gifts, subscriptions, and game purchases are final. If you believe a charge was made in error, contact AfuChat support, but refunds are not guaranteed for completed virtual currency transactions.
</Warning>
