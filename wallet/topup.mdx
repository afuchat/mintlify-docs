Topping up your AfuPay wallet converts real money into ACoins at a fixed rate of **1 ACoin = \$0.01 USD**. You can pay in your local currency — AfuPay handles the conversion automatically. The process takes under a minute using mobile money or a debit/credit card.

## Supported payment methods

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="M-Pesa" icon="mobile">
    Pay with your M-Pesa mobile wallet. Enter your phone number and confirm the STK push on your handset.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Visa" icon="credit-card">
    Pay with any Visa debit or credit card. Your card details are handled securely by the payment processor.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mastercard" icon="credit-card">
    Pay with any Mastercard debit or credit card. Supports cards issued in all supported regions.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to top up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your wallet">
    Tap the **Apps** tab in the bottom navigation bar, then tap **AfuPay** (the wallet icon) to open your wallet home screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Top Up">
    Tap the **Top Up** button on the wallet home screen. The AfuPay top-up interface will open.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select currency and amount">
    Choose your local currency from the list and enter the amount you want to add. AfuPay shows you the ACoin equivalent in real time — for example, KSh 130 ≈ 100 AC.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a payment method">
    Select **M-Pesa**, **Visa**, or **Mastercard**. For M-Pesa, enter your phone number. For card payments, enter your card number, expiry date, and CVV.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and pay">
    Review the amount and tap **Pay**. For M-Pesa, approve the STK push prompt on your phone. For card payments, the transaction is submitted automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="ACoins credited">
    Once payment is confirmed, ACoins are credited to your balance immediately and appear in your transaction history as a **Top Up** entry.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported currencies

AfuPay accepts payments in a wide range of local currencies, including:

| Region | Currencies |
| ------ | ---------- |
| East Africa | KES (Kenya), UGX (Uganda), TZS (Tanzania), RWF (Rwanda), ETB (Ethiopia) |
| West Africa | NGN (Nigeria), GHS (Ghana), XOF (Francophone West Africa), ZMW (Zambia) |
| Southern Africa | ZAR (South Africa) |
| Europe | GBP (United Kingdom), EUR (Eurozone) |
| Americas | USD (United States), CAD (Canada), BRL (Brazil), MXN (Mexico) |
| Asia-Pacific | INR (India), PKR (Pakistan), JPY (Japan), CNY (China), AUD (Australia) |
| Middle East / North Africa | EGP (Egypt) |

If your country's currency is not listed, the payment will be processed in USD.

<Note>
  Payment confirmation is usually **instant** for M-Pesa and most card transactions. In rare cases, card payments may take a few minutes to process while the payment provider verifies the transaction. If your balance does not update within 10 minutes of a successful payment, contact AfuChat support with your transaction reference number.
</Note>

<Warning>
  The **minimum top-up is 50 ACoins (\$0.50 USD)**. Top-up requests below this threshold will be rejected at checkout. There is no published maximum, but your payment provider may apply its own limits on mobile money and card transactions.
</Warning>
