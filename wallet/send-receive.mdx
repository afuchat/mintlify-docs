AfuPay makes peer-to-peer transfers instant. You can send ACoins or Nexa XP to anyone on AfuChat — whether you're splitting a bill, rewarding a creator, or surprising a friend with a Red Envelope in chat. Every transfer is recorded in your transaction history.

## Sending ACoins to another user

You can initiate a transfer in two ways:

- **By @handle** — navigate to a user's profile and tap **Send**, or open the wallet and enter their handle directly
- **By QR code** — scan the recipient's personal QR code from their profile to pre-fill their details automatically

Once the recipient is loaded, you'll see their name and avatar for confirmation. Then:

1. Choose whether to send **ACoin** or **Nexa XP** using the currency toggle
2. Enter the amount you want to send
3. Add an optional note or message (up to 120 characters)
4. Tap **Send** — the transfer completes instantly

Your balance is debited immediately, and the recipient's balance is credited in real time. You cannot send currency to yourself.

<Tip>
  Scanning a QR code is the fastest way to send money in person. Open AfuPay, tap **Scan**, point your camera at the recipient's QR code, and the send screen opens pre-filled with their details — no typing required.
</Tip>

## Requesting ACoins from a user

If you're owed money, you can send a payment request directly to another user:

1. Open the **Request** screen from AfuPay (or from a user's profile)
2. Choose **ACoin** or **Nexa XP**
3. Enter the amount and an optional reason (e.g. "Split lunch", "Bet", "Rent")
4. Tap **Send Request** — the other user receives a notification and can accept or decline

The recipient can fulfil the request with a single tap, or decline it. You'll be notified of their decision. **Payment requests expire after 48 hours** if the recipient does not respond.

## Red Envelopes

Red Envelopes let you drop a surprise bundle of ACoins into any group or individual chat. Participants tap to claim a randomised share of the total amount — first come, first served. You can send and claim Red Envelopes in any conversation, and the full history is visible in your transaction log.

<Note>
  Sending Red Envelopes requires an active AfuChat Premium subscription. All plans (Silver, Gold, and Platinum) include Red Envelope functionality.
</Note>

## Incoming payment requests

The **Money Requests** screen shows all requests in one place, split across two tabs:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Received" icon="inbox">
    Requests sent to you by other users. Each card shows the requester's name and handle, the amount and currency, their optional note, and the time elapsed. Tap **Send** to fulfil the request or **Decline** to reject it. Pending requests display a live badge count on the tab.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sent" icon="paper-plane">
    Requests you have sent to other users. You can see whether each request is pending, accepted, or declined. Tap **Cancel Request** to withdraw a pending request before the other user responds.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

The screen updates in real time — you don't need to refresh manually.

## Transaction history

Every wallet event is logged and visible in your AfuPay transaction history:

| Transaction type | Description |
| ---------------- | ----------- |
| **Top Up** | ACoins added via M-Pesa, Visa, or Mastercard |
| **Sent** | ACoins or Nexa transferred to another user |
| **Received** | ACoins or Nexa received from another user |
| **Subscription** | ACoins spent on a Premium plan |
| **Subscription switch** | ACoins spent when changing Premium tier |
| **Red Envelope sent** | ACoins placed into a Red Envelope |
| **Red Envelope received** | Your share claimed from a Red Envelope |

Each entry shows the amount, the other party's handle where applicable, a timestamp, and the optional note attached to the transfer.
