You choose the tier that fits how you use AfuChat and subscribe with ACoins straight from your wallet.

## How to Subscribe

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Premium screen">
    Go to your **Profile** and tap **Premium**, or look for the Premium shortcut in Settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your tier">
    Swipe between the **Silver**, **Gold**, and **Platinum** tabs to compare features. The price card shows the ACoin cost, its USD equivalent, and the approximate local currency value for your region.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the purchase">
    Tap **Get [Tier]** at the bottom of the screen. AfuChat shows your current balance, the cost, and your balance after subscribing so you can confirm at a glance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your subscription activates immediately">
    ACoins are deducted from your wallet and your Verified Badge and features become active right away for the full subscription period (30 days per cycle).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing or Cancelling Your Subscription

You can view your active plan — including the tier name and days remaining — at the top of the Premium screen. To cancel:

1. Open the **Premium** screen.
2. Tap **Cancel** next to your active plan status.
3. Confirm the cancellation.

<Warning>
  Cancellation takes effect immediately with no refund. Access to premium features ends as soon as you cancel — not at the end of the billing period.
</Warning>

To **switch tiers**, simply select a different tier and tap **Switch**. Your current plan cancels immediately and the new plan's ACoins cost is deducted from your wallet.

<Note>
  Premium subscriptions are paid entirely with ACoins — there is no card billing for subscriptions. If your balance is too low, AfuChat will prompt you to top up before subscribing. Visit your [Wallet](/wallet/topup) to add ACoins via M-Pesa, Visa, or Mastercard.
</Note>
