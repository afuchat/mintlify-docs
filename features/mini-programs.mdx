Mini Programs bring everyday services directly into AfuChat so you can top up airtime, pay a bill, or book a hotel room without switching between multiple apps. Every service lives under the **Pay and Services** tab and processes payments through your ACoins wallet.

## Available Services

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="📱 Airtime" icon="phone">
    Buy mobile airtime for any phone number on MTN, Airtel, Glo, or 9mobile. Choose a quick-select amount (50–2 000 ACoins) or type a custom figure. The recipient's number receives the top-up instantly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="📶 Data Bundles" icon="wifi">
    Purchase mobile data bundles for MTN, Airtel, Glo, or 9mobile. Pick from six preset bundles ranging from 1 GB (200 ACoins) to 50 GB (5 000 ACoins), each valid for 30 days.
  </Card>
  <Card title="⚡ Bills" icon="bolt">
    Pay utility and service bills directly from AfuChat. Supported bill types include Electricity, Water, TV/Cable, Internet, Waste, and Insurance. Enter your account or meter number and the amount to pay.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🏨 Hotels" icon="building">
    Browse and book hotel rooms across East and West Africa. Select a property, choose your room type (Single, Double, or Suite), set the number of nights and guests, and confirm your booking in seconds.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🎟️ Event Tickets" icon="ticket">
    Discover upcoming events — music festivals, comedy nights, tech summits, sports matches, art exhibitions, and food festivals — then buy Regular, VIP, or VVIP tickets in the quantity you need.
  </Card>
  <Card title="💸 Money Transfer" icon="arrows-left-right">
    Send ACoins to another user by their **@username** or phone number. Add an optional note describing what the transfer is for. All transfers are secured and recorded in your transaction history.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to Access Mini Programs

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Apps tab">
    Tap the **Apps** icon in the bottom navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Pay and Services">
    Select **Pay and Services** from the mini programs hub. You will see two sections: **Daily Services** (airtime, utilities, data bundles) and **Travel & Entertainment** (hotels, event tickets). Money Transfer is accessible from the banner at the top of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a service">
    Tap the service tile you want to use and fill in the required details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the fee breakdown">
    Before confirming, AfuChat shows you a clear cost summary — the service amount, the percentage fee, and the total ACoins that will be deducted from your wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your purchase">
    Tap the action button (e.g. **Buy Airtime**, **Pay Bill**, **Book Now**) to complete the transaction. Your wallet balance updates immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Payments and Fees

All Mini Program transactions are paid with **ACoins**. A small service fee (shown as a percentage) is added to each transaction and displayed in the fee breakdown before you confirm. You can tap the fee line to see a full explanation of how the charge is calculated.

<Note>
  Service availability varies by country. Airtime, data bundles, and bill payments are currently focused on African mobile networks and utility providers. Hotel and event listings reflect available inventory in supported regions. If a service is not yet available in your area, you will see a **Coming Soon** notice.
</Note>
