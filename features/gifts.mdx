Gifts in AfuChat are virtual collectibles you can send to friends, display on your profile, and trade with other users. Every gift has a rarity tier, a dynamic ACoin value, and a place in your personal collection — making gifting one of the most expressive and rewarding parts of the AfuChat experience.

## The Gift Catalog

AfuChat offers a wide range of virtual gifts, each with its own emoji, name, description, and base ACoin value. Prices are dynamic — popular or heavily traded gifts fluctuate in value based on marketplace activity, so the same gift may be worth more or less over time depending on demand.

## Rarity System

Every gift belongs to one of five rarity tiers:

  <Card title="🟢 Uncommon" icon="circle">

  </Card>
  <Card title="🔵 Rare" icon="gem">
    Sought-after gifts with meaningful ACoin value. Rare items and above can be listed on the marketplace.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🟣 Epic" icon="gem">
    High-value collectibles that stand out on any profile. Frequently traded on the marketplace.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🟠 Legendary" icon="crown">

  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Sending a Gift

You can send any gift you own to another AfuChat user directly from your collection.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your gift collection">
    Navigate to **My Gifts** from your profile or the Apps hub.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a gift">
    Tap the gift you want to send to open its detail sheet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Send">
    Tap the **Send** button, then enter the recipient's @handle. You can also add an optional personal message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the send">
    Tap **Send Gift** to complete the transfer. The gift moves instantly from your collection to theirs, and they receive a notification.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can also initiate a gift send directly from a chat conversation or a user's profile page — AfuChat pre-fills the recipient handle so you skip a step.

## Gift Marketplace

The marketplace is where Rare, Epic, and Legendary gifts are bought and sold between AfuChat users.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Listing a Gift" icon="storefront">
    Open a Rare, Epic, or Legendary gift from your collection and tap **Sell on Marketplace**. Set your asking price in ACoins and confirm. A **5% marketplace fee** is deducted from the final sale price when your gift sells.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Buying a Gift" icon="cart-shopping">
    Browse the marketplace filtered by rarity tier. Tap any listing to view the full gift details, seller information, and price history, then tap **Buy** to purchase it instantly with your ACoin balance.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Prices on the marketplace reflect real supply and demand — you can see how the asking price compares to the gift's base value and whether it's trending up or down.

## Converting Gifts to ACoins

If you'd rather have ACoins than a gift, you can convert any gift in your collection directly to its current ACoin value.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the gift">
    Tap the gift you want to convert from your collection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Convert to ACoin">
    In the detail sheet, tap **Convert to ACoin** and review the amount you will receive.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Tap **Convert** to finalise. The gift is removed from your collection and the ACoins are added to your wallet immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Pinning Gifts to Your Profile

You can pin your favourite gifts to your public profile so other users see them when they visit. Long-press any gift in your collection to toggle its pinned status, or use the **Pin to profile** option in the detail sheet.

<Note>
  Gift conversion values are based on the gift's current dynamic price minus a small processing fee. Because prices fluctuate with marketplace activity, the ACoin amount you receive may differ from the base catalog value. Rare, Epic, and Legendary gifts tend to hold higher conversion value than Common or Uncommon ones. Conversion is permanent and cannot be reversed.
</Note>
