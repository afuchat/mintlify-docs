AfuAI is AfuChat's built-in conversational AI assistant, powered by advanced language models and deeply integrated into the app. Whether you need a quick answer, help drafting a message, or want to trigger in-app actions without tapping through menus, AfuAI is always one tap away.

## What AfuAI Can Do

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Answer Questions" icon="circle-question">
    Ask anything — from quick factual lookups to in-depth research summaries. AfuAI adapts its response length to match your question.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Help With Tasks" icon="pen-to-square">
    Draft messages, write captions, crunch numbers, summarize content, or brainstorm ideas. AfuAI handles creative and analytical work alike.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Execute In-App Actions" icon="bolt">
    AfuAI can generate invoice cards, surface wallet information, and more — turning a conversation into real actions inside AfuChat.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Voice Input" icon="microphone">
    Speak your question instead of typing it. AfuAI transcribes your voice and responds just as it would to a typed message.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

AfuAI also has full awareness of your AfuChat context — it knows the platform, its features, and how things work — so you never have to explain the basics.

## How to Access AfuAI

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Apps tab">
    Tap the **Apps** icon in the bottom navigation bar to reach the AfuChat hub.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap AfuAI">
    Select the **AfuAI** shortcut. The app opens a dedicated chat thread with the assistant instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start chatting">
    Type or speak your question. AfuAI responds in seconds, and the conversation stays open so you can follow up naturally.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can also reach AfuAI directly from the home screen shortcut if you've added it to your favourites.

## Tips for Better Answers

- **Be specific.** "Summarise this in 3 bullet points" gets a better result than "summarise this."
- **Ask follow-ups.** AfuAI tracks the conversation, so you can refine or expand on any answer without repeating yourself.
- **Use it for AfuChat questions.** AfuAI is an expert on every AfuChat feature — groups, channels, the wallet, gifts, and more.
- **Try voice for long inputs.** Speaking a complex question is often faster than typing it out.

<Tip>
  To use voice input, tap the microphone icon in the message bar before sending. AfuAI uses Whisper-powered transcription to convert your speech to text accurately, even in noisy environments.
</Tip>

<Note>
  AfuAI remembers the context of your current conversation session. You can refer back to something you mentioned earlier without restating it — the assistant keeps track until you start a new session.
</Note>
