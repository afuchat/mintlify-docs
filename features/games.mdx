## ACoin Power-Ups

Every game supports ACoin-powered boosts that give you an edge when you need it most. Spend a small number of ACoins during a session to activate a power-up — for example, an extra life, a speed boost, a shield, or a board clear, depending on the game. Power-ups are optional and purely cosmetic to your score, so they enhance the experience without replacing skill.

<Tip>
  Save power-ups for high-stakes moments rather than using them early. An extra life or a board clear is far more valuable when you're deep into a run and close to a personal best.
</Tip>

## LifeSim: Life Earth

LifeSim is AfuChat's flagship mini-game and a category apart from the eight arcade titles above.

<Card title="🌍 Life Earth — Mind-Based Life Simulation" icon="earth-americas">
  You are born anywhere on Earth — one of 20+ countries, across 7 family classes. Every decision you make opens new paths and closes others. Your health, education, happiness, reputation, fitness, and wealth all evolve in response to your choices. No two playthroughs are the same, and the game never ends until your story does.
</Card>

**Key features of Life Earth:**

- **Branching paths** — each choice locks and unlocks future options, creating a genuinely unique playthrough every time
- **Legacy Score** — a composite score calculated from your stats and life milestones that places you on the global leaderboard
- **Live player count** — see how many other AfuChat users are currently living out their own simulated lives
- **Endless** — the simulation runs until the natural end of your character's life, with no arbitrary game-over screen

## Leaderboards

Life Earth features a **live global leaderboard** ranked by Legacy Score. After your session ends, your score is automatically submitted. The leaderboard displays the top 15 players along with their career, country, and current age at time of submission — giving every run a competitive edge.

<Note>
  Leaderboard rankings update in real time. Your position can change between sessions as other players complete their runs and post new scores.
</Note>
