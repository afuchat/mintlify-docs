AfuGames brings interactive entertainment straight into AfuChat. Jump into **Life Earth** — AfuChat's flagship life simulation — and watch for more arcade titles as they roll out, all without leaving the app.

## Life Earth — The Flagship Game

Life Earth is a fully playable, mind-based life simulation that sets AfuGames apart from a simple arcade collection.

<Card title="🌍 Life Earth — Mind-Based Life Simulation" icon="earth-americas">
  You are born anywhere on Earth — one of 20+ countries, across 7 family classes. Every decision you make opens new paths and closes others. Your health, education, happiness, reputation, fitness, and wealth all evolve in response to your choices. No two playthroughs are the same, and the game never ends until your story does.
</Card>

**Key features of Life Earth:**

- **Branching paths** — each choice locks and unlocks future options, creating a genuinely unique playthrough every time
- **Legacy Score** — a composite score calculated from your stats and life milestones that places you on the global leaderboard
- **Live player count** — see how many other AfuChat users are currently living out their own simulated lives
- **Endless** — the simulation runs until the natural end of your character's life, with no arbitrary game-over screen

## How to Play

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Apps tab">
    Tap the **Apps** icon in the bottom navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap AfuGames">
    Select **AfuGames** from the hub. The game screen opens with the Life Earth card front and centre.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Press Play">
    Tap the **Play** button on the Life Earth card to start a new session. Your character is born, and the first decision is yours.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Leaderboards

Life Earth features a **live global leaderboard** ranked by Legacy Score. After your session ends, your score is automatically submitted. The leaderboard displays the top players along with their career, country, and current age at time of submission — giving every run a competitive edge.

<Note>
  Leaderboard rankings update in real time. Your position can change between sessions as other players complete their runs and post new scores.
</Note>
