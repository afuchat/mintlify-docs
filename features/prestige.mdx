Prestige is AfuChat's achievement and status system that recognises how active and invested you are in the community. Your prestige tier is determined by the total ACoins associated with your account — earned through gifting, top-ups, conversions, and spending. As you accumulate more ACoins, you advance through six tiers, each unlocking new perks and exclusive items in the Status Goods shop.

## The Six Prestige Tiers

## How to Advance

Your prestige tier rises with your cumulative ACoins activity. Every ACoin you spend or receive counts toward your total. The most effective ways to climb are:

- **Top up ACoins** directly from your wallet (via M-Pesa, Visa, or Mastercard)
- **Convert Nexa (XP) to ACoins** in the Wallet screen
- **Send and receive gifts** — gifting earns ACoins for both the sender and receiver
- **Stay active** — daily activity rewards keep your rank healthy on the leaderboard

A **progress bar** on the Prestige screen shows exactly how many ACoins separate you from the next tier, so you always know how close you are to your next unlock.

## Prestige Progress Bar

The hero section of the Prestige screen displays your current tier badge and tagline, your total ACoins, your position on the **Rich List** leaderboard, and the number of Status Goods you own. Directly below is the progress bar showing your journey between your current tier and the next, with the exact ACoins gap printed in the middle.

A highlighted hint card below the bar tells you the first perk you will unlock when you reach the next tier — tap it to preview everything coming your way.

### Purchasing a Status Good

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Prestige screen">
    Tap **Prestige** from your profile or the app menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch to the Shop tab">
    Tap **Shop** in the tab bar near the top of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse and select an item">
    Each item card shows its emoji, name, where the effect appears ("Avatar ring in chats & profile", "On every message bubble you send", etc.), a short description, the required prestige tier, and the ACoin price.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Buy">
    AfuChat shows a confirmation dialog with the cost, your balance after purchase, and the full item description. Confirm to complete the transaction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Equip your item">
    After purchasing, tap **Equip** on the item card. Equipped items appear in the **Equipped** carousel at the top of the Shop tab and become visible to all other users immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Equipping and Unequipping

You can equip or unequip any owned item at any time by tapping the **Equip / Equipped** button on the item card. Equipped items are shown in a horizontal scroll strip at the top of the Shop tab for quick access.

<Tip>
  Equip high-visibility items like the **Crown Aura** or **Gold Nameplate** to make the biggest impression. Avatar effects are seen in every chat you participate in, and name effects appear next to your name on every post and comment — equipping both creates a striking, consistent presence across the entire app.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Status Goods are locked behind prestige tier requirements. If your current tier is too low to purchase an item, the button shows **Locked** and AfuChat tells you exactly how many more ACoins you need to reach the required tier. Items you have already purchased are marked **Owned** and cannot be bought again.
</Note>

## Rich List and Leaderboard

The **Ranks** tab shows a global leaderboard sorted by ACoin totals. The top three holders are displayed on a podium. Your own rank card appears at the top of the list so you can instantly see your position out of all users. A **Near Your Rank** section shows the users just ahead of and just behind you, including how many ACoins separate you from each of them — ideal for friendly competition.
