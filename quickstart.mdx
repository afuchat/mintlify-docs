Getting up and running on AfuChat takes less than five minutes. You'll create a free account, choose your unique handle, personalise your profile, and be ready to message, explore the social feed, and chat with AfuAI — all before you finish your morning coffee. Follow the steps below to get started.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the app or open the web app">
    AfuChat runs on Android and in any modern web browser.

    - **Android** — Download the free AfuChat app from the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.afuchat.app).
    - **Web** — Open [afuchat.com](https://afuchat.com) in your browser. No installation needed.

    Once open, tap **Get Started** on the welcome screen to begin.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    AfuChat uses your email address to create a secure account.

    1. Enter your email address and set a strong password.
    2. Check your inbox for a verification email and confirm your address.
    3. You'll be taken straight into the onboarding flow — no waiting required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your profile">
    The onboarding wizard walks you through five quick steps:

    1. **Display name & @handle** — Your display name is how you appear to others. Your @handle is your unique username (letters, numbers, and underscores only, minimum 3 characters). AfuChat checks availability in real time as you type.
    2. **Phone number** — Used for account recovery and finding contacts. It's never shown publicly.
    3. **Date of birth & gender** — Used for age verification and personalised recommendations. This information stays private.
    4. **Interests** — Pick at least three topics (Technology, Music, Travel, Gaming, and more) to personalise your Discover feed from day one.
    5. **Profile photo** — Upload a photo from your camera roll to complete your profile.

    Tap **Get Started** when you're done and your account is ready.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find and add friends">
    Once inside the app, head to the **Contacts** tab to start building your network.

    - **Search by @handle** — Tap the search icon and type the person's exact @handle (e.g. `@johndoe`) to find them instantly.
    - **Search by phone number** — Allow AfuChat to access your phone contacts and it will automatically show you which of your contacts are already on AfuChat.
    - **Share your profile link** — Tap your avatar, then **Share Profile** to send your AfuChat link to anyone via WhatsApp, email, or any other app.

    Tap **Add** next to a user to send a contact request, or tap their name to view their profile first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send your first message">
    Tap the **Chats** tab (the speech bubble icon at the bottom of the screen).

    - Tap the **compose icon** in the top-right corner.
    - Search for the contact you just added and select them.
    - Type your message in the text box and tap **Send**.

    Your message is delivered in real time. You can also send a voice note, attach a photo or file, react with an emoji, or send a red envelope — all from the same chat screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore everything AfuChat offers">
    Now that you're set up, take a moment to explore the rest of the app:

    - **Discover tab** — Browse your personalised social feed, watch short videos, and follow creators whose content matches your interests.
    - **AfuAI** — Tap the AI icon in the chat list (or find AfuAI in your contacts) to open your private AI chat thread. Ask it anything, any time.
    - **Wallet** — Tap your profile, then **Wallet** to check your Nexa and ACoin balances, top up, or send money to a contact.
    - **Games & Mini Programs** — Find them in the **Apps** tab to play Life Earth or access everyday services like airtime top-ups and bill payments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  AfuAI is ready the moment your account is created — no extra setup needed. Open your chat list and tap the **AfuAI** thread to start a conversation straight away. You can ask it questions, request writing help, get recommendations, and more.
</Tip>
