AfuChat brings together messaging, a social feed, an AI assistant, a digital wallet, virtual gifts, games, and mini programs — all in one place. If you're new to the platform or just need a quick answer, the questions below cover everything users ask most often. Can't find what you're looking for? [Open a support ticket](/help/troubleshooting) and our team will get back to you.

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Is AfuChat free to use?">
    Yes — downloading and using AfuChat is completely free. You can send messages, scroll the social feed, browse the AI assistant, and play games without spending anything.

    Some features use **ACoins**, AfuChat's in-app currency. ACoins let you send virtual gifts, top up a Premium subscription, and unlock power-ups in games. You earn ACoins through activity on the platform or purchase them directly via the Wallet tab using M-Pesa, Visa, or Mastercard.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is an ACoin?">
    **ACoin** is AfuChat's primary in-app currency. You use ACoins to:

    - Send virtual gifts to other users
    - Unlock Premium subscriptions (Silver, Gold, or Platinum tiers)
    - Buy power-ups in AfuChat games
    - Pay for mini program services such as airtime, data bundles, and bill payments
    - Trade on the gift marketplace

    ACoins can be purchased through the Wallet tab using M-Pesa, Visa, or Mastercard via Pesapal. They can also be earned through platform activity and by receiving gifts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is Nexa (XP) and how is it different from ACoins?">
    AfuChat runs a **dual currency system**:

    | Currency | Symbol | How you get it | What you spend it on |
    |---|---|---|---|
    | **ACoin** | Coin icon | Purchase via wallet or earn from gifts | Gifts, Premium, games, mini programs |
    | **Nexa** | XP | Earned through in-app activity and achievements | Prestige levels, achievement milestones |

    ACoins have real monetary value — you top them up with money and can use them for transactions. Nexa (XP) is an experience currency that tracks your activity and unlocks prestige levels. You cannot buy Nexa directly or convert it back to cash.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I top up my wallet?">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the Wallet tab">
        Tap **Wallet** in the bottom navigation bar.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap Top Up">
        Select the amount of ACoins you want to purchase.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose a payment method">
        AfuChat supports **M-Pesa**, **Visa**, and **Mastercard** through our payment partner Pesapal.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete the payment">
        Follow the on-screen prompts to authorise the transaction. ACoins are credited to your balance within 60 seconds of a successful payment.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Note>
      If your ACoins do not appear after 5 minutes, note your transaction reference number and submit a **Payments & ACoins** support ticket. Do not initiate another payment until the first one is resolved.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods are supported?">
    AfuChat processes payments through **Pesapal** and currently accepts:

    - **M-Pesa** (mobile money)
    - **Visa** (credit or debit card)
    - **Mastercard** (credit or debit card)

    All transactions are handled securely by Pesapal. AfuChat does not store your card details on its servers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change my @handle?">
    Your **@handle** is your unique identifier on AfuChat — it appears on your profile, in search results, and on your public profile page at `afuchat.com/@yourhandle`.

    Handle changes are supported but limited to prevent misuse. Go to **Settings → Profile** to update your handle. You may be required to pass a verification step before the change takes effect.

    <Tip>
      Choose your handle carefully — frequent changes are rate-limited to protect other users from impersonation and confusion.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my data private and secure?">
    AfuChat takes privacy seriously. Here is what you can control:

    - **Private account** — Switch your account to private so only approved followers see your posts and stories.
    - **Online status** — Hide your online status from other users.
    - **Last seen** — Control whether others can see when you were last active.
    - **Bio visibility** — Choose whether your bio appears publicly or only to followers.

    You can manage all of these in **Settings → Privacy → Account Privacy**.

    On the security side, AfuChat supports **two-factor authentication (2FA)** and OAuth sign-in via Google and GitHub. Your messages and media are stored securely and delivered over encrypted HTTPS connections.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I block someone?">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Visit the user's profile">
        Tap on the person's avatar or display name anywhere in the app.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open the options menu">
        Tap the **⋯** (more options) icon in the top-right corner of their profile.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap Block">
        Confirm the block. The user will no longer be able to see your profile, send you messages, or interact with your content.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    To review and manage your blocked users, go to **Settings → Blocked Users**. You can unblock anyone from that list at any time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I report a post or user?">
    **To report a post or story:**
    Tap the **⋯** menu on the post, then select **Report**. Choose the reason that best describes the issue and submit.

    **To report a user:**
    Visit their profile, tap the **⋯** menu, and select **Report**. Provide as much detail as possible.

    AfuChat's safety team reviews all reports. For urgent safety concerns, you can also open a **Safety & Privacy** ticket through the Support Center (tap **Help** in Settings).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I delete my account?">
    Account deletion is permanent and goes through a **five-step verification process** to protect you from accidental deletions. Here is what to expect:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Acknowledge consequences">
        You must read and check off every consequence — including loss of all posts, messages, ACoins, and premium subscriptions.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Mandatory waiting period">
        A 30-second cooldown and a required reason for leaving.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Email confirmation">
        Type your exact account email address to verify your identity.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Handle verification">
        Type your exact @handle to confirm ownership of the account.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Final phrase">
        Type a confirmation phrase, then wait 15 seconds before the delete button unlocks.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Warning>
      Once confirmed, your account enters a **30-day deletion queue**. You can cancel by logging back in within those 30 days. After 30 days, all data — posts, messages, ACoins, and media — is permanently and irreversibly erased. Premium subscriptions and ACoins are **not refunded**.
    </Warning>

    To begin, go to **Settings → Account Closure** (Danger Zone).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What countries is AfuChat available in?">
    AfuChat is available globally on Android and at [afuchat.com](https://afuchat.com) in any web browser. There are no country-specific restrictions on creating an account or using the core features.

    Payment availability depends on your chosen method. **M-Pesa** is available in supported markets (Kenya and other East African countries). **Visa and Mastercard** work internationally wherever those networks are accepted.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use AfuChat on a computer or browser?">
    Yes. The full AfuChat experience is available on the web at **[afuchat.com](https://afuchat.com)**. You can use any modern desktop or mobile browser — no installation required.

    The Android app is also available for download. An iOS app is not currently listed, but the web version works on iPhone and iPad browsers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get a verified badge?">
    A **verified badge** on AfuChat signals that an account is authentic and notable. Verification is tied to **Premium subscriptions**:

    - **Silver, Gold, and Platinum** Premium tiers unlock the verified badge alongside other exclusive features.
    - Premium is purchased with ACoins from the Wallet tab.

    <Tip>
      Organisation accounts can also receive an **organisation-verified** badge. If you represent a business or public organisation, include that context when submitting a verification request through the Support Center.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is Prestige?">
    **Prestige** is AfuChat's achievement and levelling system. As you use the app — posting, messaging, earning Nexa (XP), and completing milestones — your prestige level increases.

    Higher prestige levels unlock profile badges, cosmetic rewards, and recognition in leaderboards. Your prestige level is displayed on your **Digital ID card**, which other users can view on your profile.

    Prestige is based on Nexa (XP) earned through genuine activity — it cannot be purchased directly with ACoins.
  </Accordion>

  </Accordion>
